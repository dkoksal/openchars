About:

Welcome to OpenChars.

_ OpenChars is two things, one, it is a web based application which is used to catalog and display Cel Animations which are used to animate dynamic 2D characters, and a publicly shared archive of reusable artwork. This application is written in HTML5 and Javascript and consequently the source is available, but idealistically it is also Free Software, and a current copy of the application and the archive of art will be generated as a .zip and .tar.gz free to download, as well as a version with the application and no art and of course, the archive of art.
